Classic Waffles

Description

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning. Made with love and passion, it'll be sure to fill you up before your morning run. Easy simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
